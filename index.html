    Get to know Startup India and Ayush



    The All India Institute of Ayurveda (AIIA) has been conceived as an Apex Institute for Ayurveda. It is an Autonomous Institute under Ministry of Ayush. It aims at bringing a synergy between the traditional wisdom of Ayurveda and the modern tools and technology. The Institute has 25 specialty departments and 12 clinics with 8 inter-disciplinary research laboratories wherein several scholars have access for PG & Ph.D. programs every year. This institute also has an international collaborative centre for global promotion and research in Ayurveda.

    All India Institute of Ayurveda (AIIA) through the partnership with Startup India wants to identify and support innovations and startups working in the Ayush sectors and harness the power of new technologies.




    THEMES FOR CHALLENGES



    Problem Statement

    The key objective of the Startup AYUSH portal is to be a one-stop platform for all stakeholders (Startups, Investors, Incubators, Accelerators, Government Agencies, and Public Users) in the AYUSH system to interact and collaborate in a highly dynamic environment. The portal is directed to provide a collaborative platform for all the stakeholders of the AYUSH startups to have an interactive engagement to enhance and bolster the network. It will bring together the entire AYUSH Startup community at the global level through virtual connections, mentorship, and showcase opportunities. It will have a plethora of resources and information guides to propel everyone in their entrepreneurial journey.




    AYUSH Portal Solutions


    Information technology innovations hold the remarkable potential to empower diverse stakeholders within the Ayush sector. These innovations span a wide array of areas, from leveraging Artificial Intelligence for Ayurveda diagnostics, harnessing the capabilities of virtual reality, utilizing bio-sensors and trackers, offering cost-effective convenient care, to optimizing the use of data to enhance physician experiences during diagnosis and treatment, including patient feedback. Additionally, innovations encompass database management, methods for identifying different phases of pharmaceutical processing, and the establishment of web-based networks for tracking raw drug consumption in the pharmaceutical industry, along with interlinkages.
